<h1>about us</h1> <p> Founded in 2007, Sleepy Giant partners with creative development teams and forward-thinking IP owners to design, launch, operate, and grow entertainment publishing platforms. We have partnered with major franchises such as Tera, Pokémon Trading Card Game Online, Animal Jam, Disney’s Infinity as well as other titles by Ubisoft and Zenimax. </p>element1-1
<h1> about fofofum </h1> <p> Our goal is to give all developers, large and small, the tools and knowledge to build their best product. We’re building FFF based on our collective expertise running some of the industry’s top franchises - and we’re bringing that to you. FFF is designed to unblock your team - there’s no hardware setup and no software configuration. Just a simple and powerful service. </p>element1-4
