Cookies

“Cookies” are small bits of electronic information that a website sends to a visitor’s browser and are stored on the visitor’s hard drive. Like many websites, we employ cookies in certain areas of the Site and Client Features to allow us to provide information to make your online experience more convenient. For example, when you return to a Client Feature after logging in, cookies provide information so that the Client Feature website will remember who you are on subsequent visits, speeding up or enhancing your experience of the Client Features by, for instance, tailoring content to you. Cookies also prevent items placed in a shopping basket from disappearing by the time you checkout. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.

The use of cookies by third parties is not covered by our privacy policy.  We do not have access or control over these cookies.

Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)

We employ or our third party tracking utility partner employs a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), that help us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective.  Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users.  In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on Web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. We do not tie the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers’ personally identifiable information.

Facebook Connect or other OpenID provider

You can log in to our site using sign-in services such as Facebook Connect or an Open ID provider. These services will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share certain personal information with us such as your name and email address to pre-populate our sign up form.  Services like Facebook Connect give you the option to post information about your activities on this Web site to your profile page to share with others within your network.

Our Web site includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button and Widgets, such as the Share this button or interactive mini-programs that run on our site. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our Site. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

Additional safeguards with respect to personal information collected about children

We do not collect personal information from children under the age of 13 on behalf of our Clients unless they register with the Children’s Features as described above for all users of the Client Features. For children under the age of 13, however, a parent or legal guardian must approve their account and handle all billing on their behalf. If a child wins a sweepstakes or contest with a physical object as the reward, we may, on behalf of our Client, notify the parent at the parent’s e-mail address provided during the registration process and request that the parent provide their postal address for purposes of delivering the prize. We perform these services on behalf of our Clients and therefore such Clients may request your consent.

Many of the social aspects of the Children’s Features might result in the public disclosure of certain of your child’s personal information to third parties on the Internet, such as posting on open message boards. On behalf of our Clients, we require verification during the registration process to confirm that the parent has given permission for the child’s participation in such features through one of the following means: (i) providing his or her credit card information; or (iii) sending us, a signed consent form by fax, via PDF, GIF or other file attached to an email, or sent by SMS from a mobile device. Your children will not be able to register an account or create or post content or interact with other users on or through the Children’s Features until we receive your verifiable consent. To help prevent your children from disclosing their personal information, please make sure you talk to your children about the risks of doing so as we recommended earlier in this Privacy Policy. We perform these services on behalf of our Clients and therefore such Clients may request and provide to us your verification and consent as described above.  To request removal of yours or your child’s personal information from public areas of the sites, please contact us at info@sleepygiant.com.  In some cases, we may not be able to remove such information, in those cases if we are unable to do so we will notify you that we are unable to do so and why.
