<h1>user management</h1> <p style="font-size:16px"> Full suite of account management features. Create accounts and authenticate users, manage their profiles, leaderboards, and friends list. If we have done our job right, your team can view user purchase patterns, build cohort user acquisition groups and understand how to better monetize users.</p> <p style="color:#91C12C;font-family:Montserrat;font-size:16px;">Our parent dashboard and management tools have powered some top kid’s games. </p>element1-2

<h1>cloud computing</h1> <p style="font-size: 16px"> Our infrastructure teams manage very large scale backends for games both in private and public clouds.
